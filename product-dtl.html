CHARACTERISTICS AND ADVANTAGES

  • Suitable for small format tiles in internal areas, for floor as well as wall application.
  • Single component, fast and efficient, easy to use, only mix with water
  • Tile soaking is not needed.
  • Self-curing properties, which allows for hassle-free application with minimum labor.
  • Can be grouted within 24 hours without waiting for the moisture to evaporate

AREA OF APPLICATION

  • Interior applications for wall and floor for ceramic tiles.
  • The substrate must be structurally sound and free from all traces of loose material, laitance, oil and grease.
  • Substrates must be saturated surface dry (no standing water).
  • Pre-wet or dampen the surface with good quality fresh water if necessary.
